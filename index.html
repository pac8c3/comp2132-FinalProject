Rules

  • 3 rounds per monster - 5 levels/monsters
  • Win the fight to go to the next level
  • Reach Chrorius, The Darkmaster
  • Do not die

Scoring

  • If any of the player/monster two dice comes up as a 1 then the score for that round for the player/monster is 0
  • If the player/monster rolls a pair of the same numbers then the player/monster score is the total of the two dice times 2
  • If the player/monster rolls any other combination of dice other than the ones mentioned above then the player/monster score is the total value of the two dice
